About

London fashion photographer Lily Cyrus graduated from the London College of Fashion where she was named as one of the best graduating talents by the British Journal of Photography in their ones to watch. Since then she has gone onto shoot for many leading global brands such as Rimmel London, Ted Baker, Colourpop Cosmetics, Estee Lauder, Killian Paris, Revlon, Paco Rabanne, Milk Makeup, Carolina Herrera and Elizabeth Arden. Her work is known for a fresh lighting approach to skin with a particular focus on the glamour of beauty. Her work has appeared in several high end publications and some of the most influential digital platforms today including Glamour Magazine, Marie Claire, Grazia, WhoWhatWear and High Snobiety.Lily also regularly works with some of the most influential influencers in the fashion and beauty industry who are shaping the industry including Zoe Sugg a.k.a Zoella, Leonie Hanne and Victoria Magrath a.k.a IntheFrow. This has lead to Lily being one of the most in demand photographers creating and advising on digital content creation for many leading brands. As well as working with influencers, Lily has also partnered with leading brands such as Ted Baker, French Connection and Honor; part of the Huawei brand on her own social partnerships and branded content as an authority on photography and style. Lily Cyrus is dedicated to delivering the most professional and premium experience for stills, motion or branded content.
